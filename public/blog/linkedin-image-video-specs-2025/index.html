LinkedIn Carousel / Document Post Tips

Document posts (PDFs uploaded as carousels) are one of LinkedIn's highest-performing formats because they encourage multiple interactions as users swipe through. Each page of the PDF becomes a slide.

  • Design each slide at 1080×1080 px (square) for optimal feed display
  • Keep text large and readable — slides are small in the feed preview
  • Use a strong cover slide — it's what appears before the user clicks
  • 10–20 slides is the sweet spot; longer documents see drop-off
  • Export at 150–300 DPI for sharp text without huge file sizes
